Maintaining and improving your garden year round

Your gardens need to be regularly maintained all year round with work intensifying in the summer season. We're always on call to serve your regular garden maintenance needs. 

What we do:

  • Mowing and strimming
  • Scarifying and aerating lawns
  • Overgrowth clearance
  • Deep hand weeding 
  • Chemical weeding
  • Hedge trimming and shaping
  • Ivy and moss removal 
  • Tree and shrub pruning
  • Minor tree surgery
  • Mulching and composting 
  • Pond Care 

We're situated right at the rugged and untamed tip of the most beautiful and biodiverse regions of the UK, here you'll find species like palms and ecchiums like nowhere else that are exclusive in the UK to our local microclimate

 Summer is where green growth explodes outward and demand skyrockets for enjoyable gardens as outdoor living and recreational spaces

Most people can mow their own lawn, but with 18 years of local Cornish gardening experience and our professional setup, we are the right men to call to make sure things are done smart and properly. 

Growing any vegetables or flowers outdoors requires turning over and weeding your growing beds
just before the spring . 

Mowing the lawn needs to be done weekly in summer and less regularly in winter time. Lawns also need to be treated against invasive species and pests

Knowing what grows in your garden is important to understanding the best conditions for them to grow in and compliment their neighbouring plants

Cutting back your trees in the right way allows you to shape their growth and improve their health for the next spring season

Cutting and shaping hedges is a regular summer task due to their importance as a natural property boundary and prominent aesthetic position. 

Weeding: The best results are achieved by a mixture of hand and chemical weeding depending on species and position
